Tufts Observer: Interactive Recipes

For this project, we used Eko, a choose-your-own-adventure tool for interactive videos. We loved linking our animations together in this way and will continue to find ways to unify our artwork as a team.

Tufts Observer: Missed Connections

This semester, I joined the Observer’s new multimedia team! We create content for the publication and experiment with visual journalism and interactive web design. For our first project, we collected “missed connection” story submissions from across campus, and each of us chose one to animate and bring to life. Watch them here: Tufts Observer Missed Connections
